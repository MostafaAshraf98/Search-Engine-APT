About the PSF

The Python Software Foundation is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization entirely supported by its sponsor members and the public.  Our mission is to further the development of the Python language and its applications.

How Donations Work

If you wish to use a credit card, once on the PayPal site click the link "Don't have a PayPal account?".   A PayPal account is not required.  For your security, this website and the PSF do not acquire or retain any of your credit card data. 

Following the transaction, you will receive both a PayPal receipt and a PSF acknowledgment of your tax-deductible (USA) donation.  Please whitelist psfmember.org and check your spam bins if you don't receive the acknowledgment from the PSF.
