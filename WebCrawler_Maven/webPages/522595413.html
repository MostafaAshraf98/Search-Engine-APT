Presenting PYCON US 2022!

Conference Dates: April 27, 2022 - May 3, 2022

After two years apart, we are excited to welcome you all in person in Salt Lake City next year!

First and foremost, we want to send our very best to you and your family and friends as we all continue to navigate the challenges of COVID-19.

With the safety of our community as our top priority, we continue to monitor COVID-19 and look to venue, state, and local guidelines to make the best and most informed decisions around onsite safety and requirements.

To gather in-person again safely and protect the health of our community, we have implemented COVID-19 guidelines and protocols to be followed by all attendees at PyCon US. All attendees will be required to provide proof of full COVID-19 vaccination. During the conference, masks will be required indoors.

We ask that you please review the Health & Safety Guidelines here prior to registration.

NOTE: All Health and Safety Guidelines are subject to change due to current (April 2022) recommendations and requirements.

In the meantime, our staff and volunteers are working to bring you a fun, energizing event filled with networking, tutorials, in-person collaboration, social events, and so much more! Not to mention the great outdoors and fun activities that Salt Lake City has to offer!

Visit https://www.visitsaltlake.com/ and the PyCon US Blog for more information about Salt Lake City and all it has to offer!

Mark your calendars-we can’t wait to see you all at the Salt Palace Convention Center!

PyCon US 2022 begins April 27th, 10:00 AM EDT

Registration and Housing

PyCon US 2022 registration is now open! The registration page can be accessed via your dashboard. Be sure to head over to the Registration Information page for all the details on how to register.

When you complete your registration for PyCon US 2022, you will be able to book your hotel reservation through our official housing bureau, Orchid Events. Visit the Venue and Hotels page for more information.

To stay informed on details and announcements, be sure to create an account here on this website and opt-in to receive PyCon US News. Also, follow us on Twitter and watch the PyCon US Blog.

ALERT: We have received multiple reports of scam emails about attendee lists being received, more details can be found here.
