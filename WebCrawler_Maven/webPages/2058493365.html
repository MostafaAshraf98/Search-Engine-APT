The 200 Best Netflix Series to Watch Right Now

Updated: May 11, 2022

Looking for the best shows on Netflix? Look no further, because Rotten Tomatoes has put together a list of the 200 best original Netflix series available to watch right now, ranked according to the Tomatometer.

Find out where the most popular shows on Netflix rank, including new titles like Heartstopper claiming a spot alongside popular titles like Squid Game, Sweet Tooth, Shadow and Bone, Never Have I Ever, Bridgerton, The Queen’s Gambit, Dark, Ozark, The Witcher, Stranger Things, Black Mirror, and You.

Read also: The Best Shows and Original Movies on Amazon Prime

To be included in our list of the best of Netflix shows, titles must be Fresh (60% or higher on the Tomatometer) and have at least 10 reviews in a single season. Ties were decided by the number of reviews on each title, and then by the “average rating” (under each season’s “See Score Details”) where the number of reviews were the same. Additionally, series that were saved by the streaming service but did not originate on Netflix are included if the series meets the existing criteria. (Due to a technical issue, the series scores for some of the titles below are not currently appearing, but the titles do have scores.)

We update our list of the best Netflix series regularly, so make sure you bookmark this page to keep up with new additions to the guide.
