Outdoor Detectives

  • About Outdoor Detectives
    Outdoor Detectives is BBC Northern Ireland’s exciting new project to encourage primary aged children to get out of the classroom and choose their own local adventure.
  • Get in touch
    We want you to be detectives too and would love to share your findings.

Episodes

  • Rockpooling in Portrush
    Outdoor Detectives from Mill Strand Primary School explore rock pools on Portrush's West Strand beach.
  • Finding fascinating facts about trees in Ballyclare
    Outdoor Detectives from Fairview Primary School in Ballyclare learn how to identify the age and size of trees in their local Six Mile Water park.
  • Investigating Comber's rich history
    Andrews Memorial Primary School in Comber explore their town's amazing past.
  • Kilkeel's links to World War Two and the USA
    Grange Primary School discover the important role their local area played during the war.
