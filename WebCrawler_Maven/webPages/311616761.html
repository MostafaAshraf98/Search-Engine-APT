Snapchatters Love Apps

As a mobile-first generation, Snapchatters are actively looking for new apps:

  • 63% of Snapchatters last downloaded a new app on their phone within the past 2 weeks.2
  • 41% of Snapchatters make a purchase using an app at least once a month.3
  • 59% of Snapchatters are likely to tell their friends and family about a new app they like.4
  • Target 110+ million Snapchatters in the Casual & Mobile Gamer Lifestyle Category.5

Create Ads That Drive Snapchatters to Download or Open Your App

App Install Ads
Use app install ads to drive Snapchatters directly to your app within the app store. App install ads are available with a suite of ad formats: including Single Image or Video ads, Story Ads, and AR Lenses.
App Re-Engagement Ads
Use deep links to drive existing users back into your app. Re-engagement ads can improve retention, activate lapsed users, and drive revenue for your app. Deep link attachments are available for Single Image or Video ads, Story Ads, Collection Ads, and AR Lenses.

Measure and Optimize Your Ad Campaigns

Measure Ad Performance
Track app installs and conversion events easily within Ads Manager. We offer reporting on 20+ app events such as App Install, Purchases, Sign Ups, Level Completes, Ad Clicks, and Custom Events. Just configure your app with our approved Mobile Measurement Partners (MMPs) to measure your ad performance.

Optimize Ad Delivery
Optimize for the business goals that matter most. Snapchat offers Goal-Based Bidding to help you drive immediate growth of app installs, app purchases, and app signups with ease. Optimize towards these events once you are integrated with a MMP and a minimum number of events occur.
