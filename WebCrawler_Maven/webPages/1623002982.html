TV Premiere Dates 2022

When will the most anticipated new and returning shows and TV and streaming films of 2022 premiere? Find out with our calendar of TV and streaming premiere dates.

by Whitney Friedlander | May 17, 2022 | Comments

Rotten Tomatoes’ premiere dates calendar keeps track of the most anticipated new 2022 TV premieres and your favorite returning shows. Bookmark this page to get updates on network schedules, when the latest Netflix series launch, when Disney+ shows will premiere, what films and specials are coming your way, and more.

This list also includes titles with to-be-announced premiere dates for 2022, either because of Covid-19–related Hollywood shutdowns or because some networks and streamers enjoy reveling in our anticipation.

Premiere Date Highlights

Marvel’s latest TV series will have you seeing green. She-Hulk: Attorney at Law follows Jennifer Walters, a 30-something attorney played by Emmy winner Tatiana Maslany who also happens to be a green 6-foot-7-inch super-powered hulk. The nine-episode limited series also welcomes known MCU characters such as Tim Roth’s Emil Blonsky/the Abomination, Benedict Wong’s Wong and (not surprisingly) Mark Ruffalo’s Smart Hulk. It premieres August 17 on Disney+. Ginger Gonzaga, Josh Segarra, Jameela Jamil, Jon Bass, and Renée Elise Goldsberry also star.

HBO’s snazzy sci-fi series Westworld finally has a season 4 premiere date: June 26. The fourth season, which comes with the ominous and vague logline that it’s a “dark odyssey about the fate of sentient life on Earth,” welcomes West Side Story Oscar winner Ariana DeBose as a guest star, while returning cast members include Evan Rachel Wood, Thandiwe Newton, Ed Harris, Jeffrey Wright, Tessa Thompson, Luke Hemsworth, Aaron Paul, and Angela Sarafyan.

(Photo by Netflix)

The fifth season of Netflix’s Cobra Kai kicks off on September 9. Starring Ralph Macchio and William Zabka, the fifth season checks in with Terry Silver (Thomas Ian Griffith) as he attempts to expand the Cobra Kai empire by making his “No Mercy” style of karate the only game in town.
