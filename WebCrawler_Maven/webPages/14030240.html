Tide Tables

Predicted times and heights for more than 500 locations around the UK.

Tidal information supplied by the UK Hydrographic Office.

Inshore Waters

Specialist forecasts for coastal waters, up to 12 nautical miles offshore, around the United Kingdom, Channel Islands and Isle of Man.

Strong winds warnings in 17 regions

Summary

An area of low pressure centred to the west of the British Isles will maintain the unsettled weather conditions of late with strong winds at times in the north and west.

Shipping Forecast

The worlds longest running continuous weather forecast, the Shipping Forecast offers specialist forecasts for 31 sea areas covering the waters around the British Isles.

Gale warnings in 7 regions

The general synopsis at 1800

Low 200 miles west of Rockall 971 expected 400 miles west of Bailey 974 by 1800 Wednesday. New lows expected Rockall 995 and just east of Iceland 994 by same time. New high expected German Bight 1021 by that time

Extended Outlook

Longer-term forecasts, covering three days beyond the 24-hour Shipping Forecast, for three broad sea areas around the British Isles.

High Seas

Specialist forecasts including wind, sea state and visibility for 13 sea areas within the North Atlantic region known as METAREA I.

At 171200UTC, low 55 north 20 west 971 expected 58 north 27 west 974 by 181200UTC. New low expected 63 north 09 west 991 by same time. High 66 north 11 east 1027 expected 74 north 02 east 1024 by that time.
