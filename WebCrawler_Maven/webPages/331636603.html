Measure, Optimize, and Re-Engage With the Snap Pixel

The Snap Pixel is a tool that helps you measure, optimize, and build audiences for your ad campaigns. 

No Coding Experience Required

Install your Snap Pixel with four easy steps, no coding experience required. Simply create your Snap Pixel code, implement your Snap Pixel on your website, validate your Snap Pixel is working, and enable Pixel tracking in Ads Manager. 

Optimize Bids for Conversion Events 

Measure online events such as product views, add to cart, and online conversions with the Snap Pixel. After implementing the Snap Pixel, you can optimize towards these conversion events once the minimum threshold is met.

Re-engage Interested Snapchatters

Use Pixel Custom Audiences to re-target Snapchatters that have browsed your website with flexible lookback windows, or create Lookalike Audiences to reach Snapchatters similar to your existing customers to drive more purchases.

Ad Formats that Showcase Your Products

Use Snapchat’s made-for-commerce ad formats to reach your target audience and drive sales.

Single Image or Video Ads

Make an instant impact.
Single Image or Video Ads are full screen ads that can be used for many objectives. Simply add an attachment and enable Snapchatters to swipe up and take action.

Dynamic Product Ads

Personalize ads for your customers. 
By leveraging catalogs, the Snap Pixel, or your Mobile Measurement Partner, we can automate ad creation, while optimizing towards actions that matter most to your business, like driving purchases online or in app.

Collection Ads

Showcase a series of products.
Collection Ads appear in Stories, and showcase a series of products, giving Snapchatters a tappable way to browse.

Story Ads

Reach Snapchatters with a series of ads. 
Story Ads are a series of 3-20 Snaps that live alongside popular content tailored to Snapchatter’s preferences in the Discover section.
