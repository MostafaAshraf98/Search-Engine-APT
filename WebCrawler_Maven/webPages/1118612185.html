DevInfra

DevInfra owns most of the coding life cycle at Facebook, from the time code leaves an engineers mind until it reaches the people who use our apps. Our mission is to increase developer efficiency so that we can continue to ship awesome products quickly. We lead the industry by building innovative developer tools and automation infrastructure that are reliable and fast, ensuring that every second of engineering time is spent on the things that matter.
