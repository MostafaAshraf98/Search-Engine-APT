We're not a normal furniture company

Normal was never good enough. Frustrated by compromises between quality, affordability, and convenience, our founders started Burrow with a new approach to furniture.

SHOP AND SEE

The furniture industry hadnt changed in decades

We knew that if we wanted to make a meaningful difference, it wouldnt be as simple as putting a couch in a box and selling it online. To set a new standard, we had to focus on three fundamental changes: a modular platform, a focus on function and fashion, and an investment in community and experience.

Modular design is the innovative foundation of our furniture

Our big secret? A little bit of inside-the-box thinking. Our first task was to reverse-engineer a luxury sofa to separate into modules, and that ended up unlocking a whole menu of additional benefits.

Free shipping

Since our furniture can fit in boxes that normal delivery networks can accommodate, were able to offer free delivery on every single order which can save folks over $100 on big items like a couch.

Simple assembly

This isnt your parents flat-pack furniture. Its the next generation of modular, designed for easy, tool-free assembly that can be done in minutes.

Easy to move

Moving is inevitable. But our furniture is as easy to take apart as it is to put together, which means you can take it with you instead of leaving it by the curb.

Made to expand

Whether its adding a seat to a loveseat to make a sofa, or adding shelving units over time, our furniture is designed to grow with you.

Our design philosophy doesnt have much to do with design at all

We started Burrow because it felt like no one in the furniture industry was listening. Legacy retailers churned out one trendy, flimsy product after another, and the second they had your cash, you were on your own. But like we said, were not a normal furniture company. Were committed to designing a different experience, something completely new thats not just created for you, but with you.
