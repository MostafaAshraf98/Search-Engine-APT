Meredith International

At Meredith International we are dedicated to expanding our domestic brands across various territories by implementing innovative ideas within existing mediums. We work in close collaboration with our partners to tailor Meredith's content to resonate with targeted audiences, create commonalities, unify global readers, and further establish Meredith's already extensive portfolio.

  • International Licensing

    Our brands are well received by audiences around the world through local print editions, digital and merchandise. We offer exclusive access to original content created and produced by notable brands like People, InStyle, and Better Homes & Garden.

    • Print Editions - A combination of locally produced content and original Meredith material, these publications are sought out by domestic markets.
    • Digital - Establishing international online presence through our partners by using mediums such as social platforms and search engines.
    • Merchandise - Diversifying our portfolio by expanding outside the bounds of print and digital, further broadening our brand recognition through retail.

  • Content Partnerships

    We license Meredith copyright, trademark, and IP by providing trustworthy editorial and archival materials, which appear in numerous magazines, newspapers, and books around the world. 

    There are two ways to get started:

    • Recurring partners - we deliver curated content to our partners on a continual basis; this material is featured within our partner publications and distributed in their respective markets. Additionally, we service content aggregators by providing real-time feeds.
    • One-time permissions - we offer one-time uses of various content for appearance in movies, exhibits, articles, publications, and documentaries. 

  • Video

    Focusing on video output deals and channel launches of our PeopleTV network, our team distributes a comprehensive collection of assets across all brands. We distribute everything from various verticals of short form videos to weekly TV series for linear channels and VOD platforms. From our Four M Studios television group, we license premium productions such as Harry & Meghan's American Dream and William & Kate's Royal Anniversary.?

  • LIFE Picture Collection

    LIFE is the visual record of the 20th Century and the most prestigious private photographic archive in the United States. Containing the most iconic pictures from the last century, this collection documents and speaks to all. From 1936 to 2000, LIFE commissioned more than 10 million photographs across 120,000 stories. At it's height, LIFE's incomparable images and essays drew 1 out of 3 Americans to its pages. These original photographs and stories remain in Meredith's LIFE Picture Collection, an unprecedented cultural asset with millions of untold stories and unseen images. 

     

    LIFE offers research, gallery, licensing, and merchandising opportunities.
