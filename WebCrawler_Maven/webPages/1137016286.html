Myleene Klass: ‘Fire is something that should be discussed’

Musician Myleene Klass joined Qasa Alom on 5 Live to explain why she teaches her children to practice "different exit routes" at home.

“It’s like getting onto a plane, they will show you the nearest exits and it’s the same in life, you should know how to get yourself out of a situation as quickly as you got into it.”

“Why would you not empower your children to know what to do; to know where to go for help?”

As it’s mental health awareness week, Myleene explained that in order to protect “mental health, [people] need to not feel so helpless and not feel so out of control”.
