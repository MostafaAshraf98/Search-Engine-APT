30 Strawberry-Rhubarb Recipes to Make With Fresh Fruit

Corey Williams
By Corey Williams April 21, 2021
Skip gallery slides
Pin
rhubarb pie slice on a wooden board
Credit: Oksana Seaman

Strawberries and rhubarb are, quite frankly, a match made in summer heaven. The strawberry's delicate sweetness perfectly balances fresh rhubarb's signature tang. With this fruity power couple, you can make delicious desserts, healthy salads, easy spreads, and so much more. No matter what you're looking for, you'll find a new summertime go-to in this collection of our very best strawberry-rhubarb recipes.
