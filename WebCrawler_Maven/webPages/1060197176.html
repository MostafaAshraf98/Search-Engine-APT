Sam Srinivas
President view bio view bio

Sam is in Googles core identity team and is responsible for the secure authentication & account recovery journeys that end-users experience when they sign-in to Google.  Earlier he worked in core security & Google Cloud and provided product leadership for BeyondCorp, Googles implementation of Zero Trust access. Sam is the President of the industry-wide FIDO Alliance, which is working on standards for simpler stronger user authentication on the Internet.

Earlier, Mr. Srinivas was at WebMD, Microsoft Research and Rockwell. Mr. Srinivas has a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Stanford University and an M.S. in Mechanical Engineering from UC Berkeley.
