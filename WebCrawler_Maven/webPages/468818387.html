About Fandango

Fandango is the ultimate digital network for all things movies, serving more than 60 million unique visitors per month, according to comScore. Its portfolio features leading online ticketers Fandango, MovieTickets and Flixster; world-renowned movie review site Rotten Tomatoes; and the Movieclips multichannel network on YouTube. Fandango's movie discovery and ticketing innovations can also be found on mobile, social, AI bot and voice platforms from Apple, Facebook, Google, Amazon, and others. Video on-demand service, Vudu offers new release and catalog movies and next-day TV shows to more than 200 million connected, over-the-top (OTT) and mobile devices. In Latin America, the company operates leading online ticketers Ingresso and Fandango Latin America.

So, if you're looking to inspire, be inspired and work at the intersection of entertainment and technology look no further than Fandango.

Follow us on LinkedIn for the latest news and updates.

Fandango is an NBCUniversal Company. Benefits

  • Health

    We want our employees to feel awesome. Want proof? We offer free membership to our on premise gym and on-site fitness classes. In addition to the more traditional options in medical, dental, and vision programs, we also host the occasional massage day, in-house flue shots, and manage a private Doctor on Demand room.

  • Time Off

    We believe work life balance is essential. Full time employees get 10 vacation days, 2 floating holidays, 5 personal days, and 11 sick days a year. If that isn't enough, when the weather warms up, we often participate in a Summer Friday program.

  • Flex Schedule

    Not everyone works on the same schedule. This is why we offer flexible work arrangements and hours for certain roles.

  • Well-Certified

    Fandango is a WELL certified office! That means our office is built to positively impact our health and wellbeing through our air and water quality, ergonomic comforts, acoustics, and light design.

  • Knowledge is Power

    Enhance the skills you got, or learn something new! Engage in our tech meet-ups, or take advantage of NBCU's tuition reimbursement program.

  • 401K

    We match 100% of the first 6% (Fully vested after 2 years). We offer an additional 100% up to 1.5% (vested over 6 years) regardless of your participation in the 401(K).
